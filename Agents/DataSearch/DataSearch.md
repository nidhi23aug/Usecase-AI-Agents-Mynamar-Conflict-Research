# Data Search for Research Questions

This document describes the **dataset discovery process** used to support research questions generated by the **Gap Agent**.

The data search workflow uses the **OpenAI Data Search Agent**:

https://platform.openai.com/agent-builder/edit?version=draft&workflow=wf_6949ac60e244819082e6ed0bf22ccead09adcd4d789b8c37

The goal is to identify **NASA Earthdata CMR datasets** that can support remote sensing analysis of cropland abandonment and recultivation in Myanmar.

---

# Research Questions

The following research questions were produced by the **Gap Agent pipeline**.

## RQ1 — Spatiotemporal Pattern of Cropland Abandonment

**What is the spatiotemporal pattern of cropland abandonment and recultivation in the Myanmar conflict region?**

**H₀:**
No detectable change in abandonment/recultivation rates across conflict vs non-conflict periods or areas.

**H₁:**
Abandonment and recultivation rates show detectable temporal shifts aligned with conflict escalation or de-escalation.

### Remote Sensing Variables / Proxies

* annual or seasonal cropland state
* abandonment onset year
* recultivation onset year
* duration distributions (per pixel or object)

---

## RQ2 — Ephemerality of Conflict-Linked Abandonment

**How ephemeral is conflict-linked abandonment?**

Focus:

* duration distributions
* recultivation half-lives

**H₀:**
Abandonment persistence does not differ from non-conflict benchmark areas.

**H₁:**
Abandonment persistence differs (shorter or longer) in conflict-affected areas.

* **Spatial resolution:** ~10 m
* **Temporal coverage:** 2018–2025
* **Sensor type:** Optical remote sensing (Sentinel-2 class)

# Ranking Logic

Datasets were ranked according to metadata match:

Priority order:

1️⃣ Optical datasets with ~10 m resolution
2️⃣ Multi-temporal imagery
3️⃣ Global coverage datasets
4️⃣ Single-year derivatives

Lower rank if:

* temporal metadata incomplete
* spatial coverage limited
* not Myanmar compatible
